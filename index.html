Welcome to PNC
Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005, in Phnom Penh.


PNC offers two IT training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA) and Web

Programming (WEP) , based on a holistic educative approach including technical and soft skills,

and a personal development program.

PNC VALUE

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia values excellence, integrity, innovation, and social impact, aiming to empower youth with skills for the tech industry and society.

Trust

Build confidence by being honest, reliable, and transparent in all your actions, so others feel secure in depending on you.

Respect

Show consideration for everyone, value differences, listen actively, and treat others fairly in all interactions.

Responsibility

Take full ownership of your actions and decisions, complete your duties conscientiously, and be accountable for the outcomes.

Solidarity

Support and collaborate with others, stand together in challenges, and foster a sense of unity and community.

SPORT
Football (3PM-5PM)

PNC students enjoy football every evening as a way to relax, build teamwork, and stay healthy after classes.

Volleyball (5PM-6PM)

Volleyball at PNC is a fun way for students to relax, make friends, and enjoy teamwork after study hours.

Basketball (4PM-5PM)

Rugby (6PM-8PM)

At PNC, rugby brings excitement, teamwork, and balance between study and sport.

AEROBIC

At PNC, aerobic time is an important activity that helps students stay healthy and energetic. It usually happens in the morning, when everyone gathers together to do simple aerobic exercises with music. This activity not only keeps the body fit but also refreshes the mind, giving students more focus for their classes. Aerobic time also builds teamwork and creates a fun environment where students can enjoy moving and supporting each other.
